Who We Are

RunSocial Harrow is a friendly and inclusive running community based in Harrow, London. We welcome runners of all abilities, from complete beginners to experienced marathoners.

Our mission is to make running fun, social, and accessible to everyone in our community. Whether you're looking to improve your fitness, meet new people, or train for a specific goal, we're here to support you every step of the way.

Join us for our weekly session on Wednesday at 6pm outside Canons Park Station, Whitchurch Ln, Edgware HA8 6RN.
